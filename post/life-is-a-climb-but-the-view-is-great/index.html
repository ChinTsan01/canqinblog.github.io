Life is a climb, but the view is great.

2021-12-10 1 min read # Life and Society

(Pic by Can, Alaska, 2021/07)
It is an amazing quote by Miley Cyrus. I always thought escaping the comfort zone is similar to mountain climbing. The challenging thing is that the people are likely to fall into the comfort zone even without notice. Especially in the current days surrounded by enormous entertainments, the physical joyness has almost dominated the life. Researching is to explore the unknown and looking for the truth, which is against the human nature tending to fall into a comfort zone. I hope to keep the energy to climb all the time.
